The Critical Role Foundation

"To leave our world better than we found it"

Who is Critical Role?

Critical Role is web series put on by a group of professional voice actors. They use the game Dungeons & Dragons, to create and tell stories in a weekly show. Each "season" of the series is called a Campaign. So far they have had two different campaigns, and have just begun their third as of Oct. 21st 2021. They air on Twitch and YouTube on Thursday's at 7pm PT.

What is the Critical Role Foundation?

Their foundation is their way of trying to create good in the world outside of making entertainment online. Their mission has been to use their large platform to "create good-both in our local communities and for the world at large", this comes directly from their website. I also included a video from CRF that goes into more detail on their mission and some of the different foundations they have worked with in the past.

CRF is a non-profit organization that partners with other non-profits to help raise awareness and fund those organizations and efforts. Currently they are partnered with Shanti Bhaven to help further childrens education in India. Below is a picture that links directly to their website.
