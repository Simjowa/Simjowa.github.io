Important Education Trends

Changes of Online learning

On education trend I’ve been hearing talked about a lot is the effect that the pandemic and online learning has and is going to have on the school system. Think this is an interesting thing to theorize about and it has both ups and downs in my eyes. The world itself has been shifting online slowly for the past decade or so. From email now to online school, the shift was coming no matter what. I think there’s a lot of good that can come from online education and a lot of bad.

The Good

Online education helps to provide a lot of chances to learn for students that can’t always make it to class or have a busy schedule, this applies to both kids and adults. For instance, kids might sometimes be sick and unable to make it to school, causing them to miss important material. It’s possible in the future, it could become a standard that there’s always a virtual attendance option. As for adults, online school has always been a thing, from bachelors to masters, there’s often online colleges. With a shift in online education, these kinds of classes and opportunities might become refined and a very good option for some people.

The Bad

Online education isn’t for everyone an has its draw backs. I definitely takes a large portion of social interaction away from children who might need it, especially those in kindergarten and middle school. The younger years of school are just as much about learning how to be social as they are about getting an education. With fully online school, there’s just a portion of social interaction that is completely missing.
