Important Technology Trends

The metaverse and VR
One huge technology trend I see being important for the future is the idea of a “metaverse” and the development of VR. Recently Microsoft and Meta (previously Facebook) have been generating some buzz about the idea of a technology called metaverse. A metaverse is essentially a virtual and augmented reality-based space in which people can work, communicate, and or play games with each other in a virtual space using avatars. The idea of a virtual space has become more and more realistic as the prospect of virtual reality becomes more refined. Currently VR has reached a level where it is nearly wireless and is being applied to more than just video games. Initially metaverses were being realized through video games, with games such a VR chat being a strong example. In VR chat, you can choose or create an avatar that you then use to interact with others in a fully 3D virtual space. The game also utilizes proximity-based audio and voice chat so that communication feels even more lifelike. Meta and Microsoft have been applying similar aspects as VR chat to attempt to create their own metaverses. These companies’ idea, however, is to use it less as a game, and more as one large work space for people to come together in no matter where they are. I believe this is becoming increasingly important with the recent pandemic creating a rise in remote working and making face-to-face contact more difficult. With more developments in VR, I think it could be the next step towards bringing the work place to your home and creating a unified work space.
